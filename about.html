The Agrawal Foundation is governed by the Agrawal family — four directors who bring diverse perspectives and a common commitment to making the world better.

Our Story

Founded on Gratitude

The Agrawal Foundation was established in 2012 by the Agrawal family with an initial commitment of $1 million. The foundation was born from a simple idea: that those who have been fortunate have a responsibility to give back.

What began as a family conversation about legacy and purpose evolved into a structured philanthropic vehicle — a 501(c)(3) private foundation that could deploy resources strategically to create meaningful, lasting change.

Today, the foundation maintains over $1.25 million in assets and directs more than 90% of its annual expenditures to charitable causes, with all four directors serving without compensation.
